About Me

As an artist and an engineer it has always been my goal to pursue projects that can tie functionality and beauty into one another.

Some of the areas that I have pursued include Photography, Jewelry making, Metal working, Machining, 3d modeling, and more.

Engineering

I have found a personal calling in learning how to create the things that bring me joy, in order to make more of them and share them with others. Engineering has been a way for me to create things I am passionate about, and share them with others.

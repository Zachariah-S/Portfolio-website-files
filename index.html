About Me

As an artist and an engineer it has always been my goal to pursue projects that can tie functionality and beauty into one another. Just in case you're curious about how i manage that go check out my awesome work!

Some of the areas that I have pursued include Photography, Jewelry making, Metal working, Machining, 3d modeling, Teaching and more.
